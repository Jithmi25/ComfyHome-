About Comfy Home

Founded in 2010, Urban Furnish has been transforming houses into homes with our exquisite collection of furniture. We combine contemporary design with timeless craftsmanship to create pieces that are both beautiful and functional. Our team of skilled designers and artisans work tirelessly to bring you furniture that stands the test of time while keeping up with the latest trends.

We source only the finest materials and pay meticulous attention to every detail, ensuring that each piece meets our high standards of quality and durability. Whether you're furnishing a cozy apartment or a spacious home, Urban Furnish has the perfect pieces to reflect your personal style.

Our Mission

To create inspiring, high-quality furniture that enhances everyday living. We strive to make exceptional design accessible to everyone while maintaining sustainable practices throughout our production process.

Our Vision

To be the leading innovator in furniture design, recognized globally for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We envision homes where every piece tells a story of craftsmanship and care.
